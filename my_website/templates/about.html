About Moose

Moose is a Labrador Retriever with the perfect mix of chill vibes and endless energy. Whether he's napping in a sunbeam or sprinting after a ball, he's always 100% himself.

Personality

  • Affectionate: Will sit on you during stretches (whether you like it or not)

Quick Facts

  • Breed: Labrador Retriever
  • Favorite Activity: Walks (lives for them)
  • Favorite Toys: Anything with a squeaker, tennis balls
  • Special Move: The stretch-time sit
  • Energy Level: High (but knows how to chill)
